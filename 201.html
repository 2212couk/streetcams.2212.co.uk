Live Street Cam

Through our live cameras, we offer viewers worldwide a glimpse into life in London. Setting up and running these cameras incurs costs. If you'd like to support us, please consider donating using the provided link or purchasing something from our eBay store. Your contribution helps us maintain and improve our services. Thank you for your support! [Donate Here] [Ebay Store Here]

We don't gather any information about you as a viewer when you use this site. However, if you choose to connect your camera to our service, whether you bought it directly from us or elsewhere, we'll need to collect some details such as your name to set up your live page. If you make a donation or purchase a product from us, we may access information such as your name, email address, phone number, billing/shipping address, last four digits of your card number, card expiry date, and the amount you've donated. Additionally, if you purchase a product or service, we'll be able to see which items or services you've paid for.
